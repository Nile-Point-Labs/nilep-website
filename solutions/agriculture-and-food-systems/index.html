Agriculture and Food Systems

Agriculture and Food Systems Built for Emerging Markets.

From farm ownership and crop investment to supply chain management and direct market access, we build and operate the agricultural infrastructure that emerging markets need.

What We Do in Agriculture.

Nilep develops and operates integrated agricultural and food systems across emerging markets, enabling farm ownership, crop and livestock investment, and large-scale agribusiness operations. Our end-to-end model covers land acquisition, farm development, cultivation, livestock management, processing, storage, and distribution. Through modern farming technologies, professional farm management, and sustainable practices, we help individuals and institutions invest confidently in profitable agricultural ventures while strengthening food security and resilient supply chains. Our vertically integrated approach ensures quality control, operational efficiency, and reliable market access from production to final consumption.

Our Agricultural Services

End-to-End Agricultural Solutions.

Farm Ownership and Development

Crop and Livestock Investment

Key Commodities

What We Grow and Process.

Palm Oil

Nigeria's highest-demand vegetable oil commodity. Domestic demand consistently exceeds local supply. Nilep operates milling facilities in Cross River State.

Poultry

Fast-growing protein category with short production cycles and strong urban demand across West Africa.

Aquaculture

Fish farming addressing Nigeria's significant protein supply gap, with catfish and tilapia as primary species.

Cassava

A staple crop with strong domestic demand and growing industrial application in food processing and starch production.

Plantain

High-demand fresh and processed fruit with strong urban retail and food service demand across West Africa.
