Supply Chain Management

Supply Chain Management for Emerging Market Agribusiness.

End-to-end logistics, cold chain, storage, and distribution solutions keeping agricultural produce moving efficiently from farm to final market.

What We Manage.

Agricultural supply chain management covers the coordination of every step between farm production and final market sale, including logistics, storage, processing, and distribution, to reduce post-harvest loss and improve efficiency.

Supply chain inefficiency is one of the biggest sources of value destruction in African agriculture. Post-harvest losses, delayed logistics, inadequate storage, and fragmented distribution networks cost farmers, food businesses, and ultimately consumers enormous amounts of money every year. Nilep's supply chain management solutions address these gaps systematically, providing the logistics coordination, storage infrastructure, distribution management, and supply chain technology that allows agricultural produce to move from farm to market efficiently, safely, and profitably.

Farm-to-Market Logistics

Coordinated transport from farm to aggregation point to processor to end buyer, with route optimisation, vehicle coordination, and real-time tracking.

Cold Chain and Storage

Temperature-controlled storage and cold chain logistics for perishable produce, reducing post-harvest loss and extending the window for market access.

Off-Taker Agreement Management

Supporting the development, formalisation, and management of off-taker agreements between farmers and buyers, providing price certainty and market reliability for both sides.

Distribution Network Management

Managing the distribution of processed and packaged agricultural products to retailers, food businesses, and institutional buyers, including our direct dealer network for palm oil distribution.
