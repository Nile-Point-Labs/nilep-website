About Nilep

Nilep is a diversified, fully integrated group that provides innovative, technology-driven solutions strengthening various sectors in emerging markets. We operate across marketing and e-commerce, agriculture and food systems, artificial intelligence and blockchain technology, and supply chain management across Africa, the Middle East, Latin America, and Southeast Asia.

Nilep is headquartered at Victoria Island, Lagos, Nigeria, with operations across West Africa and active business interests across multiple emerging market regions.

Nilep works with investors, businesses, and farmers. Our clients include individual and institutional investors, SMEs and startups, agribusiness operators, and farmers looking for market access and professional support.

Investment

Private market investing is the practice of allocating capital to assets and businesses not listed on public exchanges, including agricultural operations, agribusiness portfolios, and private equity, typically through structured investment platforms, managed funds, or direct investment agreements.

Nilep currently offers the Palm Oil Production Syndicate, a structured agricultural investment product backed by active milling operations in Cross River State, Nigeria. Additional investment products are in development. Visit our invest page for current opportunities.

Investment in our Palm Oil Production Syndicate is managed through our regulated partner platform, Corre. Visit the palm oil syndicate page to review full product details, run your yield projections, and access the investment portal.

The Palm Oil Production Syndicate offers up to 10% ROI per cycle for Tier 1 investors and up to 15% ROI per cycle for Tier 2 investors, on quarterly investment cycles. Five consecutive cycles have been fully paid.

Your capital is backed by physical production assets and verified offtake arrangements. The syndicate is managed through the Corre regulated digital platform. Full terms and conditions are available on the investment product page.
