The LatAm Opportunity.

Latin America is home to some of the world's most dynamic agricultural and digital commerce economies. Brazil, Mexico, Colombia, and their neighbours are experiencing rapid growth in technology adoption, urban food consumption, and private market investment activity. We are building our Latin American presence actively and welcome enquiries from businesses, investors, and agricultural operators in the region.

Latin America produces approximately 45% of the world's soy and 25% of its beef, making it the most agriculturally productive emerging market region by export volume, with significant infrastructure gaps that represent investment and operational opportunity.
